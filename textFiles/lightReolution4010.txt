Resolution 4010-R-112911
-A resolution to fix the lights by the library
Authors: Senator Heather Engle, Senator Jimmy Momenee
Sponsor: Senator Katherine Saine
Whereas, the lights between the Carlson Library and the Student Union
are out, and
Whereas, that area is very dark, and
Whereas, many students walk between the two buildings, and
Whereas, it is dangerous to have the lights out.
Let It Be Resolved, that the lights between the Carlson Library and the
Student Union be replaced immediately.

Read 11/29/11
Passed 11/29/11
