Resolution 7010-R-112911
-A resolution to support a Red Box in the Student Union
Author: Senator Alison Wery
Whereas, Red Box would give students the opportunity to rent movies for a dollar a
day, and
Whereas, students can have a night in instead of going out, and
Whereas, students can see new movies that are cheaper than renting at another
store, Netflix, or Blockbuster.

Let it be resolved that, Student Government supports there being a Red Box
installed in the Student Union.

Read 11/29/11
Passed 11/29/11
