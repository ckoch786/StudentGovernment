Resolution 1005-R-112911
-a resolution to extend library hours on the weekend before final exams
Author: Senator Emily Kramp
Sponsor: Senator Lauren Jencen
Whereas, the library closes at 8 pm Friday and Saturday nights and,
Whereas, the library opens at 12 pm on Saturdays and Sundays and,
Whereas, students need a place to study the weekend before finals
and,
Whereas the University of Toledo wants to foster academic success
and,
Whereas, the students of the University of Toledo are paying to use the
amenities on campus,
Therefore, let it finally be resolved, that the Student Government
supports the library being open twenty four hours the Friday, Saturday,
and Sunday before finals week of each semester.

Read 11/29/11
Passed 11/29/11
