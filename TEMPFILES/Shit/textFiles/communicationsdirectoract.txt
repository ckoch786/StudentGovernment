An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for Communications Director for
the year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Communications Director shall be responsible for;
I. Communicating messages to the Student Body and community and will
report directly to the President.
II. Overseeing flyers, Facebook, Rocket Blast, UT Update, and the SG website.
III. Investigate using innovative and new methods of communication for the
benefit of Student Government.
