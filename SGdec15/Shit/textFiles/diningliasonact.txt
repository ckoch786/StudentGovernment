An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for the On-Campus Dining Liaison
for the year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government On-Campus Dining Liaison shall be responsible for;
I. Recruiting students to conduct regular evaluations of on-campus dining
services.
II. Serving on the campus dining committee to represent the student voice to
maintain appropriate food quality.
