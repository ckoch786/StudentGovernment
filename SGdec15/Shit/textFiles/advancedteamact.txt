An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for the Advanced Team, devised of
up to five members, for the year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Advanced Team shall be responsible for;
I. Attending University level meetings with the President and Vice-President.
II. Carrying out all legislation passed by the Student Senate that does not apply
to any specific cabinet position.
III. Working with the local and state government as well as the Ohio Student
Government Association as a representative of UT Student Government.

Passed- 8/23/11
