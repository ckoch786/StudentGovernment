An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for Legislative Affairs for the year
2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Legislative Affairs shall be responsible for;
I. Efficiently and frequently communicating with the Legislative Secretary of
Senate.
II. Handling legislation once it is officially passed through Student Senate.
III. Seeing that all legislation that is passed is being handled with appropriate
attention.
