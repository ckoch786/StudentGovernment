An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for Safety Initiatives Director for
the year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Safety Initiatives Director shall be responsible for;
I. Representing Student Government on the University Safety Committee.
II. Working with the Safety Committee, UTPD, and SG members to identify and
find solutions for student safety needs.
III. Negotiating a program for UTPD officers on foot and working with city officials
to increase safety in off campus areas.

Read 8/30/11, 9/06/11
Tabled 8/30/11
Passed on 9/06/11
