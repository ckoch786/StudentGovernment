An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for the Team of Membership and
Recruitment, devised of one director and two assistants, for the year 20112012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Team of Membership and Recruitment shall be responsible
for;
I. Working to recruit new students to participate in all branches of Student
Government and University Committees.
II. Keeping track of attendance at meetings and ensure the completion of all
office hours.

Passed- 8/23/11
