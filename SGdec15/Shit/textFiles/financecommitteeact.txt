An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for a Finance Committee, devised
of one director and co-director, for the year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Finance Committee shall be responsible for;
I. Approving all level 1, those expenses under $200, and level 2 expenditures.
II. Approving all budget updates.
III. Communicating and working with the Senate Finance Committee on various
requests and proposals.
IV. Efficiently running the Executive side of finance.
