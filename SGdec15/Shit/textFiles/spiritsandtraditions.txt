An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for a Spirit and Traditions
Committee, devised of one director and three assistants, for the year 20112012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Spirit and Traditions Committee shall be responsible for;
I. Planning and coordinating the UT/BG Ball Run, Football Watch Party, Lights on
the Mall, Blue and Gold Week.
II. Educating students on UT Traditions and History, promoting spirit, and
working with traditional and spirit related organizations such as Blue Crew,
Blue Key, Homecoming Commission, and the Athletic Department.

Read -8/23/11
Passed- 8/23/11
