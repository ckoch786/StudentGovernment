An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for a Diversity Committee, devised
of one director and three co-directors, for the year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Diversity Committee shall be responsible for;
I. Planning Diversity Week, beginning the second week of Fall Semester.
II. Serving as the Diversity Week Planning Committee Chair.
III. Working with and serving as a liaison to diversity related student
organizations and departments.

Passed- 8/23/11
