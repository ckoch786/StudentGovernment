An act to establish a Cabinet-level position for Technology Director for the
year 2011-2012.

Authors: Matt Rubin, Jordan Maddocks, Abbey Walsh
Purpose:
The Student Government Technology Director shall be responsible for;
I. The design and upkeep the Student Government website.
II.

To serve as the Student Government liaison for technology projects including
an Iphone application, and an SG text message service to gather data
from students.

III. Working with the Information Technology department as well as Marketing
and Communications Department.
IV. Assisting the PR Director in online advertising and student input initiatives.
