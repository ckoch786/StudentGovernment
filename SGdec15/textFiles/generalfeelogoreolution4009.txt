Resolution 4009-R-112911
-A Resolution to make students aware of how the General Fee is spent.
Author: Senator Kevin Samson
Sponsor: Senator Heather Engle
Whereas, students are unaware of how general fee is spent and,
Whereas, every student pays general fees and,
Whereas, every student should have access to how the general fee is
being spent.
Let it be Resolved, whenever general fee money is spent Student
Government recommends that a general fee logo be attached in some
way,
Let it further be resolved that Student Government suggests that any
student may submit a logo for consideration,
Finally, let it be resolved, that the general fee logo start being used by
Spring 2011.

Read 11/29/11
Passed 11/29/11
